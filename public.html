Luxuriously Handcrafted

Explore the world of beautiful carpets handcrafted with love.

Carpet Making is one of the most complex and refined crafts. Attaining the delicate balance necessary to harmonize materials with technique, design and colour, requires extraordinary sensitivity, and FatherDreamRugs manages to achieve this, after decades of commitment to the art, and a deep understanding of carpet production.

Our collection of rugs is unmatched in the world. Dive deep into the magical world of rugs, full of culture, history, and beauty. discover one-of-a-kind vintage gems, rugs handwoven from up cycled materials, revitalized heritage rugs transformed in vivid dyes, and more.
